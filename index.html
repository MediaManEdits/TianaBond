As a more recently out transgirl, my sense of fashion has been hindered by years of self-hatred and doing the bare minimum to stay alive. Therefore, I am still finding out about what goes with what, colors, shades, designs, etc - let alone accessories! My wardrobe is mix and match with plenty of things I have gotten from
thrift shops,and a few lower end clothing stores. I live in a more conservative area, so many of the pieces I am able to find at the thrifts are unfortunately not as daring as I wish, but thankfully I like the more "traditional" dresses, so its not all bad. Fashion is something I greatly enjoy (as well as my recently found love of makeup), and so I can't wait to share photos of myself in different outfits that I think look good on me!
