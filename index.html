I am a Trans Woman living in Northern Idaho with my family.
Northern Idaho is one of the most beautiful places on this planet, and it is a shame that it is filled with sexists, racists, homophobes, transphobes, nazis, and many other horrible people.
As much as I love this area, I plan on moving to the PNW proper, specifically along the coast. I am presently in need of a vehicle to live in during this intermitent timeframe, but I will settle
in Seattle, Portland, Eugene, or somewhere along the Washington/Oregon coast.
I am actively looking for a T4T polycule, preferably in Portland or Seattle. These are the two most beautiful cities I have even been to, and I look forward to one day calling either one home.



I'm just a silly girl who loves Books, Clothes, Coins, Comics, Computing, and the Outdoors! I run a preservation project which aims to save every Archie Comic. The project is (as of November 2025) on hold until sometime next year when I can dedicate more time to it. I estimate I have roughly 42% of all Floppies, Digests, and Trade Paperbacks
As a more recently out transgirl, my sense of fashion has been hindered by years of self-hatred and doing the bare minimum to stay alive. Therefore, I am still finding out about what goes with what, colors, shades, designs, etc - let alone accessories! My wardrobe is mix and match with plenty of things I have gotten from
the thrift shops, and since I live in a more conservative area, a lot of my pieces are (sadly) not that daring. Fashion is something I greatly enjoy (as well as my recently found love of makeup), and so I can't wait to share photos of myself in different outfits that I think look good on me!
